6. Verification Process Every Donee can be introduced by the Donor who is registered by the Website (“Initiating a Campaign”). The Donor shall fill a e-form reporting of the prospective Donee and his need. At “Helping Hand Gives” for the prospective Donees referred by the Donors we undergo a verification process to place before the Donors using the Website the facts pertaining to need claimed by the Donee. The verification process is a 3-part process consisting of the following: Step 1: Application Level- At this step we shall get in contact with the Donee and take primary details of the Donee along with the brief of the need and the quantum of aid required. This process shall be done telephonically by our representatives Step 2: Scrutiny of the Donee- The Donee shall meet our representatives in person and apprise of the need along with necessary documentation and relevant supporting that are required to verify the Donee. At this stage, our representative will profile the Donee based on his background, social status, genuineness of the need. This is achieved through the help of a unique questionnaire answered by the Donee. Step 3: Cross-Verification: Our representatives will visit the residence of the Donee and cross verify the data provided by the Donee. Based on the above steps, “Helping Hand Gives” shall provide a factual report of the Donee. The Donor will be able to view the factual report of the respective Donee by accessing the Wesbite through his account. The Donor may view the factual report and take the decision to donate or contribute towards the need of the Donee. “Helping Hand Gives” only collates the facts of the Donee based on the information given by the Donee. The verification process is not an endorsement or promotion of the need of the Donee by “Helping Hand Gives”. The decision to donate lies solely upon Donor.
